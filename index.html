Pete Gilbert

Seasoned IT expert with five decades of hands-on knowledge, adeptly navigating technological landscapes and solving complex challenges.

I'm an IT professional with over half a century of experience in the technology sector. I'm known for pioneering numerous innovations and contributing to the evolution of modern computing. I have a wealth of knowledge in various IT disciplines, including systems architecture, software development, networks, and project management. Adept at leading projects and mentoring the next generation of IT professionals. I've had a long career marked by significant achievements and a legacy of excellence.

Initially started as a trainee programmer learning assembler for an IBM 1401 after leaving university in 1965. These were 2nd generation mainframes with no operating system, in an isolated environment.

Progressed over the years gaining experience of operating systems, databases, many laguages and online systems.

Eventually in the nineties moved onto Microsoft machines with online access, internet and sophisticated applications with SQL databases, web sites & applications with .NET & SQL Server. Currently moving to more modern disciplines such as HTML5, CSS, Java, React and what has become known as full stack development.
